#🔥 Roastify Me

Welcome to **Roastify Me** – the sassiest Streamlit web app that serves up hilarious **roasts** or wholesome **compliments** based on your mood or what you type. Whether you're feeling fabulous, fiery, or flat, we've got the right punchline (or hug) for you.

---

## ✨ Features

- 💬 **Roast or Compliment Mode**: Choose your vibe and get a mood-based response.
- 🎛️ **Mood Slider**: Slide from grumpy to happy (-1.0 to 1.0) to control the tone of the roast or compliment.
- 🔁 **Mirror Mode**: Reflects your input back with a sassy twist.
- 🎲 **Random Mode**: Feeling indecisive? Get a surprise roast or compliment.
- 🎤 **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Hear the roast/compliment read out loud using Google TTS.
- 🎥 **GIF Reactions**: Dynamic and sassy GIFs based on the response.
- 🧾 **Roast History**: Keeps a list of all your burns and blessings.
- 📄 **Logging**: Every interaction is logged in a local `roast_log.txt` file with timestamps.

---

- Python 3.7+
- `streamlit`
